Data Deletion Instructions

App: FGB Publisher
Last updated: August 13, 2025

This page explains how to request deletion of data processed by FGB Publisher to satisfy Meta’s “User Data Deletion” requirement.

How to request deletion

  1. Email [Contact Email] with subject “Data Deletion – FGB Publisher”, include your Facebook account email/ID and any Page IDs.
  2. Revoke our app in Facebook settings (Business integrations / Apps & websites) to stop further access.
  3. Optional: Use our API endpoint when available: POST https://yourdomain.example/fgb-publisher/delete_request.

Scope

  • We delete stored tokens, connected Page configuration, queued jobs, drafts, and hosted media.
  • We may retain minimal logs required for security/audit/legal obligations.

Verification & timeline

  • We may request proof of account/Page control.
  • Deletion is targeted within 30 days of verification.
